<p><ul><li>Mechanical Vapor Compression Distiller</li><li>Thermocompression Distiller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vapor Compression Distiller market comprises two main types: Mechanical Vapor Compression Distillers and Thermocompression Distillers. Mechanical Vapor Compression Distillers utilize mechanical energy to compress vapor, increasing its temperature and allowing for efficient distillation of water. In contrast, Thermocompression Distillers rely on thermal energy from hot vapor to drive the distillation process, making them energy-efficient for large-scale applications. Both technologies are essential for desalination and water treatment, addressing global water scarcity and improving industrial processes.</p></p>
