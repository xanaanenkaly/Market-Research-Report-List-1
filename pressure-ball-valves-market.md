<p><p>The pressure ball valves market is categorized into three main types based on their operating pressure ranges. High-pressure ball valves are designed to handle pressures typically exceeding 300 psi, making them suitable for demanding industrial applications. Medium-pressure ball valves operate between 150 to 300 psi, balancing functionality and cost for moderate needs. Low-pressure ball valves, below 150 psi, are ideal for lighter applications, offering efficient flow control in residential and some commercial settings. Each type ensures reliability tailored to specific pressure requirements.</p></p>
