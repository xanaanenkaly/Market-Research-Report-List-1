<p><p>The Ultra-Fast Handheld Spectrophotometer market is comprised of various types: </p><p>1. **U-V Visible Spectrometry** measures light absorption in the UV-visible range for analyzing chemical compositions.</p><p>2. **Raman Spectrometry** utilizes inelastic scattering of light to provide molecular vibration information, useful in material identification.</p><p>3. **Fluorescence Spectrometry** detects emitted light from samples after excitation, ideal for identifying specific biomolecules.</p><p>4. **Near Infrared Spectrometry** analyzes reflected near-infrared light for agricultural and pharmaceutical applications. </p><p>5. **Others** encompass emerging techniques and hybrid methods enhancing versatility across industries.</p></p>
