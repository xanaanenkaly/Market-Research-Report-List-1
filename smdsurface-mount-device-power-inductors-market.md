<p><p>The SMD (Surface Mount Device) power inductors market comprises three main types: wire-wound, multilayer, and thin-film inductors. Wire-wound inductors utilize a coil of wire, offering high performance and efficiency, ideal for high-frequency applications. Multilayer inductors consist of multiple layers of magnetic material and conductor, providing compact size and low profile for space-constrained designs. Thin-film inductors, designed using advanced semiconductor manufacturing techniques, deliver high precision and fast response times, making them suitable for specialized power supply applications.</p></p>
