<p><strong>The Impregnated Graphite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resin Impregnated</li><li>Metal Impregnated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The impregnated graphite market is categorized into resin-impregnated and metal-impregnated types. Resin-impregnated graphite features a polymer matrix that enhances its mechanical properties and chemical resistance, making it ideal for applications requiring durability and moisture resistance. In contrast, metal-impregnated graphite incorporates metals, enhancing thermal conductivity and electrical performance, often used in high-temperature and high-load applications. Both types serve various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and electronics, providing tailored solutions for specific performance needs.</p></p>
