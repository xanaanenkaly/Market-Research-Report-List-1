<p><p>Telepresence in videoconferencing refers to technology that allows users to engage in immersive virtual meetings, simulating face-to-face interactions. The market can be categorized into two main types: Point-to-Point and Multiple Participants. Point-to-Point systems facilitate direct communication between two locations, ideal for one-on-one meetings. In contrast, Multiple Participants systems support group interactions, allowing numerous users from various locations to join a single conference, enhancing collaboration and network accessibility for businesses and organizations seeking effective communication solutions.</p></p>
