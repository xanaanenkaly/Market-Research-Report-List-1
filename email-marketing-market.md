<p><p>Email marketing can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based email marketing solutions are hosted on third-party servers, allowing businesses to easily access tools and scale their campaigns without managing infrastructure. This model offers flexibility, automated updates, and reduced upfront costs. In contrast, on-premises email marketing involves installing software on local servers, giving businesses complete control over their data and security. However, it typically requires more IT resources and higher initial investments.</p></p>
