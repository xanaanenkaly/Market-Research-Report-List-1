<p><p>The bus steering system market comprises two main types: mechanical steering systems and power steering systems. Mechanical steering systems rely on a direct connection between the steering wheel and the wheels, providing a simple, low-cost solution but requiring more driver effort. In contrast, power steering systems utilize hydraulic or electric assistance to reduce the effort needed for steering, enhancing comfort and maneuverability. Both systems have distinct advantages, catering to different needs and preferences in the bus transportation sector.</p></p>
