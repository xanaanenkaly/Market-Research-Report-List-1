<p><strong>The LDPE Geomembrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extrusion</li><li>Calendering</li><li>Blown Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LDPE geomembrane market is segmented by manufacturing processes: extrusion, calendering, and blown film. Extrusion involves melting LDPE and shaping it into sheets, offering uniform thickness and high strength. Calendering uses heated rollers to produce smooth, dense geomembranes, ideal for large-scale projects. Blown film creates thin, flexible membranes by extruding molten LDPE into a bubble, cooled and flattened. Each method caters to specific applications, balancing durability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness for uses like landfill liners, water containment, and mining.</p></p>
