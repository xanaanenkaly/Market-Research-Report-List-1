<p><p>The Tall Oil market is segmented into Softwood Tall Oil, Mixed Tall Oil, and Hardwood Tall Oil based on the source of raw materials. Softwood Tall Oil, derived from coniferous trees, is rich in rosin acids and fatty acids, making it valuable for adhesives and coatings. Mixed Tall Oil combines softwood and hardwood sources, offering a balanced composition for diverse industrial applications. Hardwood Tall Oil, sourced from deciduous trees, has lower rosin content but is used in biofuels and surfactants due to its unique properties.</p></p>
