<p><p>The infrared quartz lamp market is segmented into three main types based on wavelength: near infrared, medium infrared, and far infrared. Near infrared lamps, which emit wavelengths from 0.75 to 1.5 micrometers, are suitable for applications requiring quick heating and high intensity. Medium infrared lamps operate between 1.5 and 5 micrometers, ideal for drying and curing processes. Far infrared lamps, emitting wavelengths above 5 micrometers, are effective for deep tissue heating and various therapeutic applications. Each type serves distinct industrial and medical needs.</p></p>
