<p><p>Common mode filters with ESD protection are essential components that suppress electromagnetic interference in electronic circuits while safeguarding against electrostatic discharge. The market for these filters can be divided into two types: bidirectional and unidirectional. Bidirectional common mode filters allow signal flow in both directions, making them suitable for applications where signals can travel in either direction. In contrast, unidirectional filters permit signal flow in one direction only, making them ideal for specific circuits where signal integrity is critical in one direction.</p></p>
