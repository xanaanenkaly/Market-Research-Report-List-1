<p><p>The Low and Medium Voltage Switchgear market is primarily segmented into Gas-Insulated Switchgear (GIS) and Air-Insulated Switchgear (AIS). GIS utilizes gas insulation to achieve compact design and enhanced safety, making it suitable for space-constrained urban environments. In contrast, AIS uses air as insulation and is generally more cost-effective and easier to maintain, making it applicable in various industrial and utility settings. Both types cater to different operational needs regarding efficiency, reliability, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
