<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touch screen stylus market is divided into two main types: active and passive styluses. Active styluses feature built-in electronics, offering pressure sensitivity and functionality like palm rejection, making them ideal for detailed tasks like drawing or note-taking. Passive styluses, on the other hand, lack electronic components and rely on the touchscreen's capacitive technology. They're generally simpler and less expensive, suitable for basic navigation and interaction with touch devices. Each type serves specific user needs in the digital landscape.</p></p>
