<p><p>The microactuator market consists of various types based on their operational principles. Electrostatic actuators utilize electric fields to create motion, while electromagnetic actuators rely on magnetic fields for actuation. Piezoelectric actuators convert electrical energy into mechanical movement through piezoelectric materials. Fluid microactuators manipulate the flow of liquids or gases for precise control. Thermal actuators rely on thermal expansion to generate movement. Each type has unique advantages, making them suitable for diverse applications in industries like robotics, automotive, and biomedical devices.</p></p>
