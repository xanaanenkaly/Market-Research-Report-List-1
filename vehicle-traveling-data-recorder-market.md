<p><p>The Vehicle Traveling Data Recorder market is segmented into two primary types: single-channel and dual-channel recorders. Single-channel recorders capture data from one perspective, typically focusing on the front view of the vehicle, making them cost-effective for basic monitoring. In contrast, dual-channel recorders provide a comprehensive view by recording data from both front and rear cameras, enhancing safety and surveillance capabilities. This dual-channel option is preferred for applications needing greater detail and a broader context of driving conditions and incidents.</p></p>
