<p><strong>The E-paper Display (EPD) Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Discrete Controller</li><li>System on Chip (SoC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The E-paper Display (EPD) chip market comprises two primary types: discrete controllers and system on chip (SoC) solutions. Discrete controllers are standalone chips designed specifically for driving EPDs, offering dedicated functionality but requiring additional components for complete system integration. In contrast, SoC solutions combine the controller and associated functions into a single chip, streamlining design and reducing overall system complexity. This makes SoCs more desirable for compact, power-efficient applications, facilitating wider adoption in various electronic devices.</p></p>
