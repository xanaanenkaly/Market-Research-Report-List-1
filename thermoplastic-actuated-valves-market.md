<p><p>The thermoplastic actuated valves market comprises several valve types designed for various applications. Globe valves regulate flow with a spherical body, offering precise control. Ball valves use a rotating ball to allow or block flow, ideal for quick shut-off. Butterfly valves feature a rotating disc for efficient flow control, while diaphragm valves utilize a flexible membrane for leak-proof sealing. Gate valves provide minimal flow resistance when fully opened. Other valves in this market include check valves and pressure relief valves, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
