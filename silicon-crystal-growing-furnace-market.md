<p><strong>The Silicon Crystal Growing Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski(CZ) Method Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone(FZ) Method Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Crystal Growing Furnace market primarily comprises two key types: Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnaces and Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnaces. CZ Method Furnaces are widely used for producing single silicon crystals by melting silicon in a crucible and pulling a seed crystal upward. In contrast, FZ Method Furnaces utilize a radio-frequency coil to melt a small region of silicon under controlled conditions, allowing for higher purity and fewer defects. Both methods are critical in semiconductor manufacturing, impacting product quality and application.</p></p>
