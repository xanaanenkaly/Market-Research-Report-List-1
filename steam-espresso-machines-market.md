<p><p>The steam espresso machine market can be categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines offer complete user control, requiring baristas to finely tune extraction and steam milk manually, appealing to enthusiasts who value craftsmanship. Semi-automatic machines provide a balance, with automated water flow while allowing users to manage grind and tamping. Fully automatic machines streamline the process by automating brewing and frothing entirely, catering to those seeking convenience without compromising on quality.</p></p>
