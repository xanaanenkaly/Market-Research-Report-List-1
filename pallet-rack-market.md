<p><ul><li>Selective Pallet Rack</li><li>Drive-In and Drive-Through Pallet Rack</li><li>Push-Back Pallet Rack</li><li>Pallet Flow Rack</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pallet rack market comprises several types to optimize storage efficiency. Selective pallet racks allow easy access to individual pallets, ideal for varied inventory. Drive-in and drive-through racks maximize space by enabling multiple pallet storage in depth, with drive-through allowing access from both sides. Push-back racks use a shifting mechanism for last-in, first-out access, while pallet flow racks employ gravity to move pallets on inclined tracks for first-in, first-out inventory management. Other types cater to unique storage needs in different industries.</p></p>
