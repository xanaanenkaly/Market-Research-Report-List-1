<p><ul><li>Foley Catheters</li><li>Intermittent Catheters</li><li>Male External Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The urinary catheters market consists of three primary types: Foley catheters, intermittent catheters, and male external catheters. Foley catheters are indwelling devices used for continuous urine drainage, commonly utilized in hospitals. Intermittent catheters are designed for self-catheterization, allowing patients to empty their bladders at regular intervals. Male external catheters, also known as condom catheters, are adhesive devices applied externally to the penis, providing a non-invasive option for urine collection. Each type serves distinct patient needs and clinical applications.</p></p>
