<p><p>The Polyimide (PI) adhesives market is primarily categorized into single-component and two-component systems. Single-component PI adhesives are ready-to-use products that can be easily applied without the need for mixing, offering convenience in applications. Conversely, two-component PI adhesives require the combination of a resin and hardener before application, providing enhanced bonding strength and chemical resistance. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, with single-component options favored for speed and ease, while two-component systems are preferred for more demanding environments.</p></p>
