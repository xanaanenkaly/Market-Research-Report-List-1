<p><p>The fishing line market is primarily divided into two main types: monofilament fishing line and winding fishing line. Monofilament fishing line is a single filament line known for its flexibility, ease of handling, and affordability, making it popular among beginners and casual anglers. Winding fishing line encompasses braided and fused lines, which offer superior strength and sensitivity, ideal for more experienced anglers targeting larger fish or fishing in challenging conditions. Each type serves specific fishing needs and preferences.</p></p>
