<p><p>The residential hobs market includes primarily two types: gas hobs and induction hobs. Gas hobs operate using natural gas or propane, providing instant heat control and traditional cooking familiarity, appealing to many home chefs. Induction hobs, on the other hand, use electromagnetic energy to directly heat cookware, allowing for faster cooking times and precise temperature control. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, influencing factors like efficiency, safety, installation requirements, and overall cooking experience within modern households.</p></p>
