Resistance Strain Type sensors measure deformation by detecting changes in electrical resistance as the material strains. Piezoresistive Type sensors work similarly but leverage the piezoresistive effect, wherein a material's resistance changes under mechanical stress. Both types are crucial for applications in structural health monitoring, robotics, and flexible electronics, offering enhanced sensitivity and accuracy due to the properties of quantum dots in sensor technology.</p></p>
