<p><p>Cellulose diacetate, a semi-synthetic polymer derived from cellulose, is available in powder and flaky forms, catering to diverse industrial applications. The powder type is finely ground, offering ease of dissolution and uniform mixing, making it ideal for coatings, films, and adhesives. Flaky cellulose diacetate, with its larger, irregular particles, is preferred for applications requiring controlled release or structural integrity, such as in cigarette filters and textiles. Both types are valued for their biodegradability, thermal stability, and versatility in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
