<p><ul><li>Wetting & Dispersion Additives</li><li>Defoaming Agents</li><li>Rheology Modifiers</li><li>Flow Additives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterborne coating additives market includes various types designed to enhance coating performance. Wetting and dispersion additives improve surface coverage and pigment distribution, ensuring uniform application. Defoaming agents reduce foam formation during application, preventing defects. Rheology modifiers adjust the viscosity and flow characteristics, enabling better control during application. Flow additives enhance the leveling and smoothness of the final coat, improving aesthetic appeal. Together, these additives contribute to superior durability, appearance, and application efficiency of waterborne coatings.</p></p>
