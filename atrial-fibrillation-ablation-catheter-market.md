<p><p>The atrial fibrillation ablation catheter market comprises three main types: Radiofrequency Ablation Catheters, Cryoablation Catheters, and Other Catheters. Radiofrequency ablation catheters use heat to destroy abnormal heart tissue, while cryoablation catheters employ extreme cold for the same purpose. Other catheter types may include laser or microwave-based ablation devices, each with unique mechanisms and applications. These technologies are crucial for treating atrial fibrillation by targeting and eliminating faulty electrical pathways in the heart.</p></p>
