<p><ul><li>Nebulizing</li><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Heat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric diffuser market comprises three main types: nebulizing, ultrasonic, and heat diffusers. Nebulizing diffusers use pressurized air to disperse essential oils into fine mist, preserving their therapeutic properties. Ultrasonic diffusers utilize high-frequency vibrations to create a mist, blending water with oils for a subtle scent and increased humidity. Heat diffusers gently warm the oils, releasing aroma but altering some beneficial compounds. Each type caters to different consumer preferences for fragrance intensity, therapeutic effects, and ease of use.</p></p>
