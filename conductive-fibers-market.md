<p><p>Conductive fibers are specialized materials designed to conduct electricity, often integrated into textiles, fabrics, or composites. These fibers are made by coating or embedding conductive materials like silver, copper, or carbon into traditional fibers such as polyester or nylon. They are widely used in applications such as smart textiles, wearable electronics, electromagnetic shielding, and medical devices, offering flexibility, durability, and conductivity.</p><p>The Conductive Fibers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for smart textiles and wearable technology. As industries continue to prioritize connectivity and smart solutions, the conductive fibers market is poised for significant growth in the coming years.</p></p>
