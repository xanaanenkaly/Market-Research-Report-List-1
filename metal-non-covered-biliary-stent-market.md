<p><ul><li>Absorbable</li><li>Non Absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metal Non-Covered Biliary Stent market is divided into two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable stents. Absorbable stents are designed to degrade over time, providing temporary support for bile ducts before being safely absorbed by the body. In contrast, non-absorbable stents are made from permanent materials that remain in place indefinitely, maintaining bile duct patency without being absorbed. Each type serves specific clinical needs, with absorbable stents offering flexibility and non-absorbable stents providing long-term solutions.</p></p>
