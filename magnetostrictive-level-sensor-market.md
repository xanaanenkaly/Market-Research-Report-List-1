<p><p>Magnetostrictive level sensors are used for measuring liquid levels through magnetostrictive technology. The market is divided into two main types: Continuous Level Monitoring and Point Level Monitoring. Continuous Level Monitoring provides real-time data, allowing for constant tracking of liquid levels in tanks and reservoirs, enhancing operational efficiency. In contrast, Point Level Monitoring detects specific levels, often triggering alarms or control systems when the liquid reaches a predetermined point, ensuring safety and preventing overflows or dry runs.</p></p>
