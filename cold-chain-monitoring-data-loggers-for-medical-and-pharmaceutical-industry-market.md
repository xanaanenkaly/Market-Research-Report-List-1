<p><p>Cold chain monitoring data loggers are essential for maintaining the integrity of temperature-sensitive products in the medical and pharmaceutical industries. Offline data loggers record temperature and humidity data during transport without real-time monitoring, ideal for less frequent shipments. Cellular connected data loggers utilize cellular networks to provide real-time data, enabling immediate alerts on temperature deviations. Wireless connected data loggers use Wi-Fi or Bluetooth for continuous monitoring and easy integration with existing systems, ensuring compliance and product safety during storage and transport.</p></p>
