<p><p>The industrial centrifuge machines market consists of various types designed for specific separation processes. Sedimentation centrifuges separate solids from liquids based on density differences. Filtering centrifuges utilize filter media for solid-liquid separation. Basket centrifuges hold solids while allowing liquids to escape. Scroll screen centrifuges use screw-like mechanisms for continuous separation. Peeler centrifuges automate the discharge of separated solids, while pusher centrifuges utilize pressure to enhance separation efficiency. Other types encompass specialized or hybrid techniques tailored to unique industrial applications.</p></p>
