<p><p>The freight elevator market comprises primarily two types: hydraulic freight elevators and electric freight elevators. Hydraulic freight elevators use a fluid-driven system, providing smooth and quiet operation, ideal for transporting heavy loads in low-rise buildings. In contrast, electric freight elevators utilize electric motors and pulleys, offering faster speeds and greater efficiency, suitable for mid- to high-rise applications. Both types are designed to meet specific cargo handling needs, ensuring safety and reliability in various industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
