<p><p>The aluminum extruded products market consists of three primary types: mill-finished, powder-coated, and anodized. Mill-finished products have a raw, unprocessed surface, showcasing the natural aluminum appearance. Powder-coated items are coated with a powdered paint, providing vibrant colors and enhanced durability against corrosion and wear. Anodized products undergo an electrochemical process that thickens the natural oxide layer, improving corrosion resistance and allowing for various color finishes. Each type caters to distinct aesthetic preferences and functional requirements in construction and manufacturing.</p></p>
