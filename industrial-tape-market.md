<p><p>The industrial tape market comprises various types, each serving specific applications. Non-adhesive industrial tape offers protection and insulation without adhesives. Single-sided adhesive tapes feature adhesive on one side, suitable for securing materials or providing cushioning. Double-sided adhesive tapes bond two surfaces together, ideal for mounting or laminating. Transfer tape consists of a thin adhesive layer that transfers easily to surfaces, enhancing versatility. Other types include specialty tapes for unique applications, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
