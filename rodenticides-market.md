<p><strong>The Rodenticides Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anticoagulants</li><li>Non-Anticoagulants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rodenticides market is divided into two primary types: anticoagulants and non-anticoagulants. Anticoagulants prevent blood clotting in rodents, leading to internal bleeding and death over a few days, making them effective for long-term management. Non-anticoagulants, such as phosphides and bromethalin, act more rapidly, providing quick kills. Each type serves different pest control needs, with anticoagulants often preferred for ongoing infestations, while non-anticoagulants are chosen for immediate results, catering to varying market demands and regulatory considerations.</p></p>
