<p><p>The medium voltage surge arresters market is categorized into three main types: embedded type, wall-mount type, and built-in type. Embedded type surge arresters are integrated within electrical equipment for space-saving and enhanced protection. Wall-mount type devices are installed on walls, offering easy accessibility and maintenance. Built-in type surge arresters are incorporated directly into circuit breakers or transformers, providing streamlined protection without requiring additional space. Each type serves to safeguard electrical systems against voltage spikes, ensuring reliability and safety.</p></p>
