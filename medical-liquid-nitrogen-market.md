<p><p>The medical liquid nitrogen market comprises two main types: on-site gas and bottled gas. On-site gas systems generate nitrogen directly at healthcare facilities, providing a continuous supply while reducing transportation costs and ensuring purity. In contrast, bottled gas involves transporting pre-filled nitrogen cylinders to medical facilities, offering convenience and flexibility but necessitating regular inventory management and deliveries. Both types serve essential roles in medical applications, including cryopreservation, surgical procedures, and laboratory uses, facilitating critical patient care and advanced research efforts.</p></p>
