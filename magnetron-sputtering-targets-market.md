<p><p>The Magnetron Sputtering Targets market includes two primary types: DC Magnetron Sputtering and RF Magnetron Sputtering. DC Magnetron Sputtering uses direct current to create a plasma, enabling the deposition of conductive materials like metals and alloys onto substrates. RF Magnetron Sputtering employs radio frequency for non-conductive materials, such as ceramics and oxides, ensuring efficient thin-film deposition. Both methods are widely used in industries like semiconductors, optics, and coatings, offering precise, high-quality thin-film production with excellent adhesion and uniformity.</p></p>
