<p><ul><li>Fuel Based Auxiliary Heaters</li><li>Electrical / PTC Auxiliary Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive auxiliary heater market comprises two primary types: fuel-based auxiliary heaters and electric/PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) auxiliary heaters. Fuel-based heaters utilize combustion of traditional fuels like diesel or gasoline to generate heat, providing efficient warmth in vehicles, particularly useful in cold climates. In contrast, electric/ PTC heaters rely on electrical resistance to produce heat, offering quick and convenient solutions without emissions. Both types enhance passenger comfort and vehicle performance during low-temperature conditions, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
