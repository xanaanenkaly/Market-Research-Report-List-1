<p><p>Metallized Rollstock PEP Film is a specialized packaging material made from polyethylene terephthalate (PET) that has been coated with a thin layer of metal, typically aluminum. This film is widely used in food packaging, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods due to its excellent barrier properties, moisture resistance, and aesthetic appeal. The metallization enhances product visibility and shelf life while reducing the overall weight of packaging. </p><p>The Metallized Rollstock PEP Film Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.6% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for sustainable and efficient packaging solutions.
