<p><p>5G Non-Standalone (NSA) architecture utilizes existing 4G LTE infrastructure while integrating new 5G components. The market types include:</p><p>1. **Femtocell**: Small, low-power nodes for indoor usage, enhancing coverage in homes or small businesses.</p><p>2. **Pico Cell**: Slightly larger than femtocells, these are deployed in dense urban areas for improved capacity and coverage.</p><p>3. **Micro Cell**: Mid-sized base stations catering to larger coverage areas, often used in urban settings.</p><p>4. **Macro Cell**: Large, powerful cell towers providing extensive coverage over significant areas, essential in the backbone of mobile networks.</p></p>
