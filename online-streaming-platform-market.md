<p><strong>The Online Streaming Platform Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SVOD (Subscription-based Video on Demand)</li><li>TVOD (Transactional-based Video on Demand)</li><li>AVOD (Advertisement-based Video on Demand)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The online streaming platform market consists of three primary models: </p><p>1. **SVOD (Subscription-based Video on Demand)** offers unlimited access to a library of content for a recurring fee, allowing users to watch at their convenience.</p><p>2. **TVOD (Transactional-based Video on Demand)** allows users to purchase or rent individual titles, providing flexibility for viewing specific content without a subscription.</p><p>3. **AVOD (Advertisement-based Video on Demand)** is free for users, supported by ads that generate revenue, thus allowing access to content without payment.</p></p>
