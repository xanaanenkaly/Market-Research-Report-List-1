<p><strong>The Power Assisted Steering PAS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power Assisted Steering (PAS) encompasses various technologies that enhance vehicle steering precision and ease. The three main types are:</p><p>1. **C-EPS (Column Electric Power Steering)**: Uses an electric motor mounted on the steering column, offering efficient energy use and design flexibility.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **P-EPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering)**: Integrates the electric motor directly with the steering pinion, providing improved feedback and a compact design.</p><p>3. **R-EPS (Rack Electric Power Steering)**: Features the motor on the steering rack, delivering enhanced responsiveness and direct steering feel. Each type aims to improve driving comfort and vehicle handling while reducing weight and energy consumption.</p></p>
