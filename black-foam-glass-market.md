<p><ul><li>Closed Cell</li><li>Open Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Black Foam Glass market is primarily categorized into two types: closed cell and open cell foams. Closed cell foam features a dense structure where gas bubbles are trapped within individual cells, offering superior insulation and moisture resistance, making it ideal for applications in construction and refrigeration. In contrast, open cell foam has interconnected cells, allowing for better airflow and sound absorption, often utilized in packaging, automotive, and acoustic applications. Both types fulfill specific niches based on their unique properties.</p></p>
