<p><p>The Hardware Security Module (HSM) market is categorized into three primary types based on connectivity: LAN-based, PCIe-based, and USB-based HSMs. LAN-based HSMs connect through local networks, providing centralized security for multiple clients, ideal for enterprises. PCIe-based HSMs integrate directly into servers, offering high-speed performance suitable for applications requiring low latency. USB-based HSMs are portable and user-friendly, enabling secure key management for personal devices and smaller setups, catering to various needs across different sectors and organizations.</p></p>
