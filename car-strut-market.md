<p><p>The car strut market encompasses various types, including coilover struts, gas charged struts, and pneumatic struts. Coilover struts combine shock absorbers and springs, allowing adjustable ride height for performance tuning. Gas charged struts use pressurized gas to enhance responsiveness and reduce fade, ideal for improved handling. Pneumatic struts utilize air pressure for variable damping, adapting to load changes and providing a smooth ride. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to diverse consumer preferences in vehicle suspension systems.</p></p>
