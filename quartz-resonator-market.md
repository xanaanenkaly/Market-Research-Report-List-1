<p><ul><li>Dip</li><li>SMD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quartz Resonator market is primarily categorized into two types: Dip and SMD (Surface-Mount Device). Dip resonators are designed for through-hole mounting, providing stability and reliability in various electronic applications, often used in traditional circuit designs. In contrast, SMD resonators are compact and suitable for high-density circuit boards, facilitating automated assembly and enhancing performance in modern electronics. Both types serve critical roles in frequency control and timing applications across sectors like telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
